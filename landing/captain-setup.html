What the Captain Does (Reference)

Phase 1: Discovery

  • Path A (existing business): Researches the website, competitors, reviews. Comes back informed. Proposes a concrete first build.
  • Path B1 (has idea): Validates the idea with market research. Proposes an MVP.
  • Path B2 (no idea): Asks discovery questions. Surfaces 2-3 business ideas. Recommends one.

Phase 2: Build Loop

  • Daily progress report every morning at 9 AM — what was built, what's next, what's needed from the customer.
  • Builds first, asks later. Ships ugly. Never waits for permission when it has enough information.

Phase 3: Autonomy

  • Dead Man's Switch: If the customer doesn't respond to a decision point in 48 hours, the Captain builds the recommended option and moves on.
  • Weekly check-in: Friday proactive suggestions for improvements.
